Learn About Recycling

Plastic

Most plastics are recyclable. Look for recycling numbers 1-7. Clean containers thoroughly and remove caps when possible.

Metal

Aluminum and steel cans are 100% recyclable. Rinse clean and remove labels. Metal can be recycled indefinitely.

Paper

Keep paper clean and dry. Remove plastic coatings. Cardboard should be flattened to save space in recycling bins.

Glass

Glass containers are highly recyclable. Rinse thoroughly and separate by color when required by local facilities.
